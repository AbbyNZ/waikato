Events

The Waikato region is known as a leading events centre. International sporting matches such as rugby, cricket, and netball are held here. The region is also known for its annual events such as Balloons Over Waikato and the annual Kawhia Kai Festival. Music and art festivals, international expos, and concerts makes Waikato's event calendar exciting.

  • New Zealand International Film Festival

    19 Aug - 13 Sept 2015
    Hamilton, Waikato

    The New Zealand International Film Festival will be held at Lido Cinema, Hamilton, Waikato from 19th of August to 13th of September 2015.

  • Skycity Breakers vs Sydney Kings

    09 Sept 2015
    Claudelands Arena, Hamilton

    The Skycity Breakers are set to return to Claudelands Arena on Wednesday, 9th of September, taking on the Sydney Kings.

  • Aroha Cruise In

    02 Oct - 04 oct 2015
    Te Aroha, Matamata-Piako

    The Aroha Cruise In is a classic cars show in Te Aroha, Waikato. See some of the country's classic cars from the 2nd to the 4th of October.

  • Gallagher Great Race

    12 Sept - 13 Sept 2015
    Hamilton, Waikato

    The Gallagher Great Race is an annual 4.8km long rowing race on the Waikato river. The race is between the men's eight from the University of Waikato, New Zealand and a prominent university team (or teams) from outside New Zealand.
