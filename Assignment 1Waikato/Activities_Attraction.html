The Waikato region is a culturally diverse region. By the 2000s, Waikato had become a destination for adventure tourism.

The region has many attractions and activities that will suit all ages, tastes, and budgets. From a relaxing day out to an adrenaline-rush adventure. You will never be bored in Waikato.
